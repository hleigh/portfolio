About

During the length of my college career I have had the opportunity to work with a range of clients. I started out in my field with a digital marketing internship at Boys Town Press in 2019 and from there I went on to join MaverickPR, the student-run PR agency at the University of Nebraska-Omaha. During my summer with MaverickPR I worked on designs for SWAG, brochures, re-branded logos, wrote a few feature stories, and more. Other clients I have had include the Douglas County Health Center and Delta Sigma Pi, the professional business fraternity.
